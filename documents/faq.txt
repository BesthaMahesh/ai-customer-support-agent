AI Customer Support Agent - Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and we will send you a password reset link. Follow the instructions in the email to set a new password. If you do not receive the email within 10 minutes, please check your spam folder or contact support.

Q: How can I contact customer support?
A: You can reach our customer support team via email at support@company.com or call us at 1-800-555-0199. Our support hours are Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM EST. We typically reply to emails within 24 business hours.

Q: How long does shipping take?
A: Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping takes 1-2 business days. International shipping can take 7-14 business days depending on the destination country and customs clearance times.

Q: Do you offer international shipping?
A: Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. You can view the list of supported countries during the checkout process. Note that international shipments may be subject to import duties and taxes, which are the responsibility of the customer.
